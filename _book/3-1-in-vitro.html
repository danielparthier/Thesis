3.1.1 Slice Preparation

For slices animals (n = XX PV-Cre mice, n = XX ChAT-Cre mice ) were deeply anaesthetised using isoflurane decapitated, the brain removed and quickly transferred to ice-cold slicing solution. sucrose artificial cerebral spinal fluid (SACF). SACF contained 87 NaCl, 26 NaHCO3, 10 Glucose, 50 Sucrose, 2.5 KCl, 1.25 NaH2PO4, 0.5 CaCl2, 3 MgCl26H2O 400 Âµm horizontal slices were produced using a vibratome (VT1200S, Leica Biosystems, Wetzlar, Germany), then transferred and stored in an interface chamber for up to 1-6 h. Slices were perfused with ACSF (119 NaCl, 26 NaHCO3, 10 Glucose, 2.5 KCl, 1 NaH2PO4, 2.5 CaCl2, 1.3 MgCl26H2O) and oxygenated during the whole period.
